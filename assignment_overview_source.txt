In this project, you worked on designing and developing the front-end of a (fictitious) non-profit healthcare platform called StayHealthy, aimed at improving access to medical services in remote and underserved areas with limited healthcare infrastructure. As part of StayHealthy’s Go Digital initiative, the platform was envisioned to help patients connect with doctors anytime and from anywhere through a web-based solution.

You were responsible for designing the user interface and user experience using Figma, ensuring accessibility, usability, and a patient-friendly layout. You developed static and responsive web pages using HTML, CSS, and React.js, focusing on clean design, responsiveness across devices, and an intuitive navigation flow.

The application was structured as a modern front-end web solution and prepared for deployment to support StayHealthy’s broader mission of maximizing digital reach. Throughout the project, you followed front-end best practices, emphasized responsive design principles, and ensured the website was deployment-ready for future integration with back-end services.
